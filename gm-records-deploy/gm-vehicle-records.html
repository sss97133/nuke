Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of records can I receive?

Depending on the vehicle and availability, records may include build sheets, shipping records, dealer invoice copies, factory options, and production information.

How far back do GM's records go?

GM maintains various records dating back to the early 1900s, but availability varies by brand, model, and year. The most complete records typically exist for vehicles from the 1960s forward.

Why use your service instead of contacting GM directly?

Our service handles all the paperwork, follows up on your request, and ensures proper documentation. We have experience with the specific requirements needed for successful retrieval, saving you time and potential frustration.

How will I receive my records?

Records are delivered digitally via secure email. Physical copies can be mailed for an additional fee.
