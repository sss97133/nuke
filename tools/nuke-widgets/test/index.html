@nuke1/widgets

3 embeddable tools for any website. Paste the snippet, get vehicle intelligence.

1. Vision — AI Vehicle Identification

Drop a photo of any vehicle. YONO identifies make/model/year, assesses condition, and returns market value — in under 5 seconds. Zero cost per image.

<nuke-vision api-key="nk_pub_..."></nuke-vision>

Visitors enter a VIN or year/make/model. They get an instant valuation with comparable sales. You get demand signals and inventory intelligence.
