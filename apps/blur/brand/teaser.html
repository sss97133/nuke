Blur A Nuke Product

Blur

The private photo app that organizes itself.

Free. Anonymous. Nothing ever leaves your phone.

The problem

A life worth showing — buried in ten thousand photos.

The idea

Everything softened. Only the one you meant, in focus.

Hand someone your phone. Flip straight to it. The rest stays blurred.

What it is
01

Private by nature

No account. No cloud. No upload. It works in airplane mode, because everything happens on your phone.

02

Quietly ordered

It organizes itself, and finds the photos you missed — completing the albums you started. You never sort by hand again.

03

Shown with intention

Flip to exactly what you mean to show. Everything else softens away. No scrolling, no second-guessing.

What it is not

No feed. No cloud. No clutter. Only your photos, at their best.

The promise

Your photos. Your phone. Nothing leaves.
