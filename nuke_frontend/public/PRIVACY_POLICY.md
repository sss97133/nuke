## 1. Introduction

NUKE LTD ("NUKE," "we," "us," or "our") operates the NUKE platform at n-zero.dev (the "Platform"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our Platform.

By using the Platform, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and login credentials
- **Identity Verification:** Government-issued ID, Social Security Number (for investment features), date of birth
- **Financial Information:** Bank account details, payment card information, income and net worth declarations (for accredited investor verification)
- **Vehicle Data:** Photos, videos, documents, VIN numbers, ownership records, and descriptions you upload
- **Communications:** Messages, support requests, and feedback you send us

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, clicks, time spent on Platform
- **Location Data:** General geographic location based on IP address
- **Cookies:** Session cookies, authentication tokens, and analytics cookies

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Authentication Providers:** If you sign in via Google or other OAuth providers
- **Financial Services:** QuickBooks data when you connect your accounting software
- **Payment Processors:** Transaction confirmations and fraud screening results
- **Identity Verification Services:** KYC/AML verification results

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- **Provide Services:** Operate the Platform, process transactions, manage your account
- **Verify Identity:** Comply with KYC (Know Your Customer) and AML (Anti-Money Laundering) requirements
- **Process Investments:** Facilitate securities offerings and manage investor records
- **Communicate:** Send service updates, security alerts, and marketing communications (with consent)
- **Improve Platform:** Analyze usage patterns, debug issues, develop new features
- **Prevent Fraud:** Detect and prevent fraudulent or illegal activity
- **Legal Compliance:** Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
- **Financial Reporting:** Generate tax documents (1099s) and regulatory filings

---

## 4. How We Share Your Information

### 4.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Personal Information

We never sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

### 4.2 Service Providers

We share information with vendors who help us operate the Platform:
- **Cloud Infrastructure:** Supabase, Vercel, AWS
- **Payment Processing:** Stripe, bank partners
- **Identity Verification:** KYC/AML service providers
- **Accounting Integration:** QuickBooks (Intuit)
- **Analytics:** Privacy-focused analytics tools
- **Communication:** Email service providers

All service providers are bound by data processing agreements requiring confidentiality and security.

### 4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by:
- Court orders, subpoenas, or legal process
- Law enforcement requests
- Regulatory examinations (SEC, FINRA, state regulators)
- Protection of our rights, safety, or property

### 4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

### 4.5 With Your Consent

We may share information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

## 5. Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained for the life of your account plus 7 years for regulatory compliance
- **Investment Records:** Retained permanently as required by securities laws
- **Financial Data:** Retained for 7 years per IRS requirements
- **Usage Logs:** Retained for 2 years
- **Marketing Data:** Until you opt out

You may request deletion of certain data as described in Section 7.

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

- **Encryption:** TLS 1.3 for data in transit; AES-256 for data at rest
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access, multi-factor authentication for staff
- **Monitoring:** Continuous security monitoring and audit logging
- **Compliance:** SOC 2 Type II compliant infrastructure (Supabase)
- **Incident Response:** Documented procedures for security incidents

No system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but commit to promptly notifying affected users of any breach.

## 7. Your Rights

### 7.1 Access and Portability
You may request a copy of your personal data in a portable format.

### 7.2 Correction
You may update or correct inaccurate information through your account settings or by contacting us.

### 7.3 Deletion
You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements. Some data (investment records, tax documents) cannot be deleted due to regulatory obligations.

### 7.4 Opt-Out
You may opt out of:
- Marketing emails (unsubscribe link in each email)
- Non-essential cookies (browser settings)
- Data sharing for analytics (contact privacy@n-zero.dev)

### 7.5 Do Not Track
We do not currently respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals.

## 8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

- **Right to Know:** Categories and specific pieces of personal information collected
- **Right to Delete:** Request deletion of personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out:** Opt out of sale of personal information (we do not sell)
- **Non-Discrimination:** We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights

To exercise CCPA rights, contact privacy@n-zero.dev with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.

By using the Platform, you consent to this transfer. We implement appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.
