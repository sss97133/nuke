E: Badge Portals — Each Badge Opens a Different World

Every badge is a door into a different data shape. Click PORSCHE and you don't see more Porsche cards — you see the Porsche market: trend direction, volume, avg estimate, hottest model. Click STEAL ▼95% and you see WHY it's a steal: the comparable sales, confidence breakdown, methodology. Click MONTEREY, CA and you see local market density. No data repeats from parent to child — each layer branches into new soil. ESC to close.
