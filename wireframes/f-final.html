F: Post-Critic Synthesis

Three tiers of progressive disclosure. Tier 1 (always visible): price position, heat, data quality — the 2-second scan. Tier 2 (click to expand): sources, what changed, contested, rarity, inspection coverage — the intelligence layer. Each badge opens a unique data shape. Academic names replaced with plain language. The ontology powers the signals — you feel it, you don't read it.
