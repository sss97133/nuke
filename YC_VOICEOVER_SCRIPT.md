# YC Video Voiceover Script
## ~50 seconds. One take. Voice Memos. Done.

---

**[Over dashboard/driving shot]**

"AI can write code, generate images, pass the bar exam. But the only API for fixing an old car is still human interface. You need the guy who owned it. The forum post from 2008. The mechanic who remembers the job."

**[Over engine closeup + you working]**

"We built the data layer that changes that. 758,000 vehicles. 627,000 data points. 181 extraction tools I built by hand. Our system knows what a vehicle is worth, what work it needs, and what the ROI is on doing that work."

**[Over paint panel + parts on bench]**

"And then the bot hires the human. It vets the job -- cost, ROI. It vets the worker -- their history, their reliability. The bot finds the opportunity and the right person to do it. The human does what only humans can do -- the physical work."

**[To camera -- separate clip, standing in the shop]**

"I'm [name]. AI is incredible at identifying opportunities. But the last mile in these trades will always be human hands. I'm not replacing tradespeople -- I'm building bots that hire them. Our only bottleneck is workspace."

---

## The line that sticks:
**"The only API for fixing old cars is still human interface."**

## Tips
- Talk like you're explaining to a buddy, not presenting
- Let the "bot hires the human" concept land -- it's the hook
- ~50 seconds total. The to-camera closer is ~12 seconds.
- Record VO and closer separately. Drop both in ~/Desktop/yc-video/
