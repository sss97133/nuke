>> SHIELDED CABLE CONNECTORS (4 items)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The CKP, CMP, KS1, KS2 wires use 2-conductor shielded cable. The shield drain
wire must be grounded at the ECU end (M130 B15/B16 SEN_0V) and left floating at
the sensor end. No additional connector needed — the ICT pigtails above include
the sensor-side termination. The ECU-side terminates into the Superseal pins.
