# Appendix C: Desert Performance Invoice Analysis

## Invoice #1190 — March 9, 2019

**From:** Desert Performance, 676 Wells Rd, Boulder City NV 89005
**To:** Howard Barton (reference vehicle, not the K5 Blazer)
**Total:** $23,970
**Build:** Complete Motec M130 + PDM30 wiring system on a custom coupe

### Why This Invoice Matters

This is the only invoice we have that itemizes Motec components at actual dealer cost. Every other price for Motec hardware is either MSRP (inflated) or "call for pricing" (opaque). Desert Performance is a Motec dealer and builds complete harnesses — this invoice shows what the parts actually cost when purchased by a professional shop.

### Price Intelligence Extracted

**Motec dealer pricing** (lower than MSRP):
- M130: $3,500 (MSRP ~$4,500)
- PDM30: $3,140 (MSRP ~$4,000)
- LTCD: $844 (MSRP ~$1,000)
- GT101: $80 (MSRP ~$100)
- RBD-190: $300 (MSRP ~$400)

**Labor rate:** $65/hr — significantly below market for Motec-certified work (typical $85-125/hr). This is Boulder City NV pricing, not LA or Bay Area.

**Harness construction:** $6,000 for engine + chassis = about 40-50 hours of fabrication at $65/hr (materials included). This is professional-tier TXL/DTM construction, not consumer-grade.

**Total Motec system cost (parts only):** $8,919
**Total build cost including labor:** $23,970

### Application to K5 Blazer Estimate

The K5 Blazer BOM uses this invoice as a pricing anchor. When the compute engine prices an M130 at $3,500, that's the Desert Performance dealer price, not MSRP. When it estimates 55 hours of installation at $65/hr, that's based on this invoice's labor rate plus 10% for the larger squarebody chassis.
