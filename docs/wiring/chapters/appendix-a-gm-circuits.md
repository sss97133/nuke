# Appendix A: GM Electrical Circuit Identification Table

Source: 1973 Chevrolet Light Duty Truck Service Manual, Section 8C, Pages 46-51

## About This Table

GM assigned every electrical circuit a unique number and standardized wire color. This table covers circuits 2 through 502. It is the authoritative reference for factory wire colors on 1973-1987 C/K series trucks (squarebodies).

**Database table:** `gm_circuit_identification` — 219 circuits extracted from OCR'd service manual text.
