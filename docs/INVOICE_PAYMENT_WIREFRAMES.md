## 📱 Key Design Principles

### **1. No Login Required (Public Access)**
- Client can view and pay invoice via link only
- No account creation barrier
- One-click payment confirmation

### **2. Multiple Payment Methods**
- Show all available methods clearly
- QR codes for mobile payment apps
- Copy-to-clipboard for manual entry
- "Mark as paid" for cash/check

### **3. Mobile-First**
- Responsive design for all devices
- Large touch targets
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Minimal scrolling

### **4. Onboarding Hook**
- After payment: "Create account to track invoices"
- Show benefits: receipt storage, payment history
- Optional but visible

### **5. Trust Signals**
- Shop name and contact visible
- Invoice number prominently displayed
- Vehicle details for verification
- Payment confirmation with receipt
