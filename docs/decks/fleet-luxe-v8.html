Players

  • Ford Brand Team — authorize activation, co-op budget
  • US Dealer — 126 Heritage Editions in stock nationally
  • Freight Forwarder — Savannah/Charleston to Gustavia
  • SBH Collectivite — 8% octroi de mer on CIF
  • FBM — receives, registers, maintains, sells to hotel
  • Fouquet's — hotel placement, guest access
  • L'Officiel — media, content, luxury verification
  • L'Agence — PR, concierge, client relationships

Bottlenecks

  • SOGUAVA — current Ford distributor, also sells Suzuki. May need to bypass.
  • Export tax structure — no GA form for international buyers. VLVA (NV dealer) available as fallback.
  • Carte grise — SBH registration is opaque. FBM handles locally.
  • Ford franchise law — must go through a dealer. Brand team can influence which one.
  • Seasonal timing — high season Nov-Apr. Vehicles arrive by October.

Fleet Luxe 12

Documentation

What we need to close.

US Side

Dealer

MSO

Manufacturer's Statement of Origin. Untitled vehicles, export-bound.

Tax

ST-4 or Export Affidavit

VLVA route uses ST-4. Direct export needs dealer affidavit.

Export

EEI / AES Filing

72-hour advance notice to CBP. Customs broker handles.

Shipping

Bill of Lading

RORO or container. Savannah/Charleston to Gustavia.

SBH Side

Customs

Octroi de Mer

8% on CIF. Paid to Collectivite at Gustavia port.

Registration

Carte Grise

FBM handles. Requires customs receipt, ownership proof, insurance.

Insurance

Local Fleet Policy

Mandatory. FBM sources through local insurer.

Compliance

Controle Technique

French inspection. New vehicles typically exempt initially.

Fleet Luxe 13

Acquisition Strategy

Three paths. Same destination.

Path A: Ford Brand Placement

Ford provides vehicles through activation budget. Dealer instructed by corporate. Zero friction.

Best case. Requires brand team buy-in.

Path B: Direct Purchase

Buy from a cooperative US dealer. Export sale, no registration. Dealer-trades to assemble colors. ~$220-238K.

Most likely. Needs dealer who'll do export.

Path C: VLVA Intermediary

VLVA (NV dealer, DLR000053625) buys via GA Form ST-4. Resells to FBM. Proven paperwork.

Fallback. Adds NV tax risk on resale.
