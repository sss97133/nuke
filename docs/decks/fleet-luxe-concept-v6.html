Players

  • Ford Brand Team — Adam Mohamed, Donte Jenkins. Authorize activation, potentially subsidize via marketing co-op.
  • US Ford Dealer — Source of vehicles. 126 Heritage Editions in stock nationwide. Dealer-trades to consolidate colors.
  • VLVA (backup) — NV dealer license DLR000053625. Only needed if dealer requires dealer-to-dealer sale for tax structure.
  • Freight Forwarder — Port of Savannah or Charleston to Gustavia. RORO or container. AES/EEI export filing.
  • SBH Customs / Collectivite — 8% octroi de mer on CIF value. Carte grise registration.
  • FBM Automobiles — On-island operator. Receives, registers, maintains, displays. Airport frontage location.
  • Fouquet's Saint-Barth — Hotel partner. Provides placement, guest access, brand alignment.
  • L'Officiel St Barth — Media partner. Editorial content, social, first automotive brand.
  • L'Agence St Barth — Creative production. Deck, campaign, visual identity.

Bottlenecks

  • SOGUAVA (Guadeloupe) — Current Ford distributor for French Caribbean. Controls allocation. Also distributes Suzuki. May resist or need to be bypassed.
  • GA/SC Tax Exemption — No standard form for international export buyers. ST-8 covers US interstate only. Requires dealer cooperation or VLVA as intermediary.
  • Carte Grise — Vehicle registration in SBH can be difficult. FBM handles locally but process is opaque for US-origin vehicles.
  • Ford Franchise Law — Ford can't sell direct. Must go through a dealer. But brand team can influence which dealer cooperates.
  • Seasonal Timing — SBH high season is Nov-Apr. Vehicles need to arrive before November for maximum impact.

Fleet Luxe 13

Documentation

What we need to close.

US Side

Dealer

MSO (Manufacturer's Statement of Origin)

Untitled vehicles. MSO transfers from dealer to buyer. No state title issued — vehicles are export-bound.

Tax

GA Form ST-4 or Export Affidavit

If using VLVA: ST-4 (out-of-state dealer). If direct export: dealer affidavit + shipping documentation. GA DOR confirmation pending.

Export

EEI / AES Filing

Electronic Export Information filed through Automated Export System. 72-hour advance notice to CBP. Customs broker handles. ITN assigned per vehicle.

Shipping

Bill of Lading

RORO or container booking. Savannah or Charleston to Gustavia. Proof of export for tax documentation.

SBH Side

Customs

Declaration + Octroi de Mer

CIF value declared at Gustavia port. 8% duty paid to Collectivite. Commercial invoice + bill of lading required.

Registration

Carte Grise

Vehicle registration with SBH authorities. FBM handles as local operator. Requires customs clearance receipt, proof of ownership, insurance.

Insurance

Local Policy

Mandatory vehicle insurance. FBM sources through local SBH insurer. Fleet policy preferred for 4+ vehicles.

Compliance

CT (Controle Technique)

French vehicle inspection equivalent. May be required for registration. New vehicles typically exempt initially.

Fleet Luxe 14

Acquisition Strategy

Three paths to the same destination.

Path A: Ford Brand Placement

Ford provides vehicles through activation budget. Dealer is instructed by corporate. Zero tax friction. Vehicles arrive as marketing assets, not inventory.


Best case. Requires Ford brand team buy-in.

Path B: Direct Dealer Purchase

Buy from a cooperative dealer (Bozard FL, Sarasota FL, or any volume store). Export sale, no registration. Dealer-trades to assemble colors. ~$220-238K.


Most likely path. Requires dealer who'll do export paperwork.

Path C: VLVA Intermediary

VLVA (NV dealer, DLR000053625) buys via GA Form ST-4 tax-free. Resells to FBM. Adds a link in the chain but uses proven dealer-to-dealer paperwork.


Fallback. Adds cost and NV tax risk on resale leg.
