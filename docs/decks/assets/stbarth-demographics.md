| Taxi service | ~40 licensed taxis island-wide. No app. Call and wait. |
| Public transit | NONE. |

| Dominant rental vehicles | Suzuki Jimny, Dacia Duster, Smart ForTwo, Moke, Chinese electric buggies |

**Key insight for Ford:** Every single visitor to St. Barths rents or is provided a vehicle. There is no alternative. The market is 100% penetration. The only question is WHICH vehicle.

## Why Ford / Why Now

### American Buyers Are Already Here
- 60-70% of high-season visitors are American
- They already own or are considering Broncos
- They are experiencing the vehicle in the most aspirational setting possible
- A Ford Ambassador can close while the guest is IN the product

### No Ford Presence Exists
- SOGUAVA distributes Ford commercial vehicles in French Antilles but has minimal SBH presence
- Zero Ford dealer, zero Ford signage, zero Ford vehicles in rental fleets
- Complete greenfield market for brand awareness and retail

### Every Vehicle Is Visible
- 25 sq km island, 1 main road, 14 beaches, 50+ restaurants
- A Bronco Sport is seen by hundreds of people per day
- Annual impressions per vehicle (estimated): 100,000-300,000 visual encounters
- 4 Broncos = 400K-1.2M annual impressions on the most exclusive island in the Caribbean

### The Buyer Context
- Guests are relaxed, on vacation, spending freely
- They drive the product for 5-7 days (the ultimate test drive)
- No purchase pressure — the Ambassador is there for questions, not for closing
- Purchase decision happens organically: "I want one of these at home"
- Ford Ambassador facilitates US-side sale through any Ford dealer
