# Rental-to-Purchase Research — Citable Data for Ford Pitch

## THE KILLER CITATION: Ford GT350-H (1966)

Ford + Hertz + Shelby created the Rent-a-Racer program. 1,003 GT350-H units placed in Hertz airport locations at $17/day.

- 1965 Shelby production: **562 units**
- 1966 Shelby production: **2,386 units** — Hertz cars = **42% of total production**
- **4x production increase** directly attributable to the rental program
- Program revived 4 times: 1966, 2006, 2016, 2022 (three-year partnership)
- 2022: GT500-H at $399/day in Las Vegas, Phoenix, Miami, Orlando, Tampa — all vacation markets

Sources: MotorCities National Heritage Area, Hagerty Media, Motor Authority
