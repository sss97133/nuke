# Luxe Fleet v9 — Stakeholder Review Synthesis

**5 simulated stakeholder reviews** | April 2026
**Verdict: Strong concept, not ready to send. V10 requires structural changes.**

---

## UNANIMOUS FINDINGS (All 5 partners agree)

### 1. SPLIT THE DECK
Every partner said it independently. The current deck tries to speak to Ford, Fouquet's, L'Agence, L'Officiel, and FBM simultaneously and speaks clearly to none of them.

**Action:** Build two decks:
- **Deck A — Ford Activation** (for Dearborn): ROI, impressions, demographics, dealer mechanics, brand activation language. Slides 10-13 (supply chain, docs, acquisition paths) belong here.
- **Deck B — Hospitality & Experience** (for L'Agence network, Fouquet's, L'Officiel): Guest experience, exclusivity, aesthetic proposition, editorial narrative. No dealer license numbers, no tax form references, no SOGUAVA.

### 2. INSURANCE & LIABILITY IS THE #1 GAP
Every single partner flagged this. The deck has one bullet point ("FBM sources local fleet policies") where it needs a full slide.

**Required new slide: "Insurance & Liability Framework"**
- Policyholder structure (FBM? Luxe Fleet? Hotel?)
- Coverage types: guest injury, property damage, third-party, theft
- Guest waiver/damage deposit process
- Alcohol + driving protocol (Fouquet's called this "the most predictable risk")
- Crisis communications chain
- French hospitality liability law (responsabilite du fait des choses)
- Estimated cost: EUR 16,000-28,000/year for 4 vehicles

### 3. NO CLEAR "ASK"
The deck describes a system but never says what each partner is being asked to do or get. Every partner asked: "What do you want from me?"

**Required new slide: "The Ask" (per-deck version)**
- Ford deck: Specific dollar request, vehicle count, term, what Ford gets in return
- Hospitality deck: Specific cost to hotel, what hotel gets, term, exclusivity

### 4. ANNUAL OPERATING COSTS MISSING
Slide 11 shows acquisition cost ($273K) but not the $150K-270K/year in operating costs (FBM's detailed breakdown). Every financial partner needs the full picture.

**Required: Revise economics to show Year 1 TCO and Year 2+ recurring**

### 5. NO TIMELINE
No dates anywhere. FBM says 12 weeks minimum, must start by July for October delivery. Hurricane season complicates barge scheduling.

**Required: Gantt-style timeline from commitment to vehicles-on-ground**

1. ROI model with impressions projections and media value estimate

2. Fee structure: retainer, introduction fees, event fees, exclusivity

## VOICE & TONE CHANGES

### Hospitality Deck
- "Every vehicle is content" → Delete. The content happens naturally if the vehicle is beautiful enough.
- "From landing to Instagram" → "From landing to return." End with the guest, not with Ford's analytics.
- "Zero content value" → "No aesthetic consideration" or "No brand alignment"
- "Ford sees it" → Delete from guest journey. Ford's reward is not the guest's concern.
- "70% buyers are male" / "0 Caribbean activations" → Ford deck only. Not relevant to luxury audience.
- "The operational backbone" → Rewrite in service language, not startup language.
- "Amplify" → "Distinguish" or "Elevate"

### Ford Deck
- Lead with the demographic expansion opportunity and Caribbean market penetration
- Position Heritage Edition's $59.5K price as "charming heritage character" not luxury price parity
- Honest competitive framing against G-Wagon/Range Rover organic island presence
- Remove the five-way partner equity framing — Ford is the brand principal

## WHAT EVERY PARTNER ENDORSED

Despite the gaps, every partner validated the core concept:

1. **The gap is real.** Ultra-luxury hospitality curates everything except the vehicle.
2. **Heritage Edition is the right product.** Design language, colorways, and retro positioning genuinely fit.
3. **FBM as local operator solves the hardest problem.** Named, capable, airport-front, 30 years on-island.
4. **The partner ecosystem is logical.** Each role is distinct and necessary.
5. **"The vehicle is the experience"** — strong closing line. Keep it.

**Ford would take a meeting.** Fouquet's would take a second meeting. L'Officiel is genuinely interested. L'Agence wants to present it but can't yet. FBM wants to sign but needs real terms.

The concept has earned the right to exist. The deck has not yet earned the right to be sent.
