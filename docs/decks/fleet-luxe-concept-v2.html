Fleet Luxe 01

The Problem

A $3,000 per night hotel.
A $15,000 rental car.

Every luxury destination has the same gap. Guests arrive at world-class properties and drive forgettable fleet vehicles. The hotel controls every detail of the experience — the linens, the fragrance, the playlist — but not the vehicle.

In St. Barths, the most exclusive island in the Caribbean, guests drive Suzuki Jimnys and Mokes. The vehicle is the one piece of the luxury experience that nobody owns.

What the guest experiences

  • $3,000/night villa at Fouquet's Saint-Barth
  • Private chef, butler service
  • Helicopter transfer from St. Maarten
  • Curated wine, art, music
vs

What the guest drives

  • Suzuki Jimny from airport counter
  • Dented Moke with no AC
  • Random rental, no brand alignment
  • Zero curation, zero experience

The Solution

for luxury destinations.

Fleet Luxe is the first luxury vehicle fleet operator purpose-built for hotels, resorts, and destinations. We source, curate, import, maintain, insure, and rotate vehicles — so the hotel delivers a complete experience.

Fleet Luxe 10

Platform

Powered by Nuke.

Every vehicle in the Fleet Luxe network lives as a digital twin on the Nuke platform. Condition tracking, provenance, maintenance history, photo documentation, valuation — the complete vehicle lifecycle managed through data.

Digital Twin

Every vehicle has a complete digital record — specs, condition, photos, service history. The hotel sees fleet status in real time.

Condition Tracking

AI-powered photo analysis tracks vehicle condition across 41 zones. Damage is documented before it becomes a problem.

Rotation Engine

Nuke manages fleet freshness — when to rotate vehicles, when to service, when to replace. The fleet always looks showroom.

Fleet Luxe 11

Activation Timeline

From concept to villa driveway.

Phase 1

Ford Brand Alignment

Engage Adam Mohamed and Bronco brand team. Present activation concept. Secure 4 Heritage Edition Broncos through brand placement budget.

Phase 2

Hotel Agreement

Formalize fleet management contract with Hotel Barriere Fouquet's Saint-Barth. Define placement locations, guest access model, branding guidelines.

Phase 3

Import & Position

Export from US East Coast port. Handle SBH customs (8% octroi de mer). Register vehicles. Position at hotel and FBM airport display.

Phase 4

Content & Launch

L'Officiel St Barth editorial shoot. Social content across all partner channels. Ford reposts. Other hotels inquire. Scale begins.

Phase 5

Vintage Layer

Source and restore vintage Broncos for permanent FBM airport display. The showroom becomes a destination. Classic meets Heritage Edition.
