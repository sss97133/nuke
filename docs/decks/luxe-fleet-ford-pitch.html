Luxe Fleet 01

The Thesis

St. Barths is where brands go
to be seen and validated.

The Hermès boutique in Gustavia is the highest-grossing Hermès boutique in the world. Eden Rock is the most recognized hotel destination in the Caribbean. Every luxury brand that matters has a presence on this 25 km² island — not because of transaction volume, but because being seen there validates the brand at a global level.

The automotive landscape is the exception. It's flooded with Jimnys, Mokes, and Chinese electric buggies — because the rental companies answering the call optimize for margins, negotiating whether a commission is $1,500 or $2,000. Nobody from the brand side has shown up.

Ford's experiential marketing budget for a single activation outweighs the entire cost of all vehicle sales on the island.

The rental companies see vehicles. We see media real estate. Hermès didn't open in Gustavia to sell more bags. They opened there because every bag sold is seen by the right person, in the right light, and validates the brand at a scale no advertising can match.

Ford should think about St. Barths the way Hermès does.

Luxe Fleet 07

Honest Competitive Assessment

G-Wagons are already there.
That's not the play.

Mercedes G63 AMGs park outside Eden Rock daily. Range Rovers appear at Cheval Blanc. Cayennes are ubiquitous. These are organic — owners and wealthy renters bring what they know.

Heritage Edition Bronco doesn't compete on price ($60K vs $200K G63) or status signaling. It competes on character. And it's already winning — ~20 Broncos sold on-island through back-channel imports, without any Ford support.

  • G-Wagon — says "I'm rich." Everyone has one.
  • Range Rover — says "I'm tasteful." Corporate.
  • Heritage Bronco — says "I'm interesting." Doors off, heritage design, open air on a Caribbean road. Already on the island. Already chosen.

The demand is proven. People are paying a premium to import Broncos through a painful process with no Ford support and $1,000 service calls. Formalize the channel and this scales immediately.
