# Investment-Grade Due Diligence Report Structure

## Overview

The due diligence report is now a **comprehensive, investment-grade analysis** suitable for $100K-$10M investment decisions. It provides deep analytical insights across all critical investment dimensions.

## Report Sections

### 1. **Executive Summary** 
3-4 paragraph overview covering:
- Business model and market position
- Key financial indicators
- Investment appeal
- Primary risks
- Written for busy investors who need quick understanding

### 2. **Investment Thesis**
2-3 paragraph investment thesis answering:
- Why invest in this business?
- What makes it attractive?
- What's the value proposition?
- What's the investment case?

### 3. **Investment Grade**
Overall investment grade: **A+ | A | B+ | B | C+ | C | D | F**
- Based on comprehensive risk/reward analysis
- Considers all factors: financial, operational, market, competitive

### 4. **Business Overview**
- **Description**: Comprehensive 2-3 paragraph business description
- **Business Model**: Primary revenue model identification
- **Revenue Streams**: All identifiable revenue streams (sales, service, parts, fees, etc.)
- **Specializations**: Specific areas of expertise
- **Services Offered**: Detailed list of services/products
- **History & Background**: Founding story, years in business, milestones, growth trajectory

### 5. **Market Analysis**
- **Market Positioning**: How they position themselves (premium, budget, niche, etc.)
- **Target Market**: Detailed customer demographics and psychographics
- **Market Size Estimate**: Addressable market size if inferable
- **Competitive Landscape**: Analysis of competitive environment
- **Competitive Advantages**: Key differentiators and competitive moats
- **Market Share Indicators**: Signals of market position

### 6. **Financial Analysis**
- **Financial Signals**: Visible indicators (inventory size, price ranges, transaction volume)
- **Pricing Strategy**: Pricing approach (premium, value, competitive)
- **Revenue Model Details**: How money is made, pricing structure, margins
- **Cost Structure Indicators**: Signals about cost structure
- **Profitability Indicators**: Signals of profitability
- **Scale Indicators**: Signals of business scale

### 7. **Operational Assessment**
- **Operational Capabilities**: Core in-house capabilities
- **Facilities & Equipment**: Facilities mentioned (paint booth, dyno, showroom, service bays)
- **Team Size Indicators**: Signals of team size
- **Technology Stack**: Technology mentioned (online platform, CRM, inventory management)
- **Operational Efficiency Signals**: Signals of operational efficiency

### 8. **Risk Assessment**
- **Key Risks**: Array of identified risks with:
  - Risk description
  - Severity (high/medium/low)
  - Mitigation strategies
- **SWOT Analysis**: Comprehensive SWOT
  - Strengths
  - Weaknesses
  - Opportunities
  - Threats

### 9. **Growth & Opportunities**
- **Growth Potential**: Assessment of scalability and expansion opportunities
- **Expansion Opportunities**: Identified opportunities (geographic, service line, market segment)
- **Market Trends Alignment**: How they align with market trends

### 10. **Reputation & Credibility**
- **Reputation Indicators**: Awards, media coverage, industry recognition
- **Credibility Signals**: Certifications, licenses, partnerships, years in business
- **Customer Testimonials Indicators**: Signals of customer satisfaction

### 11. **Investment Considerations**
- **Investment Notes**: Key considerations for investors
- **Valuation Considerations**: Valuation factors (revenue multiples, asset value, growth trajectory, comparables)
- **Exit Strategy Indicators**: Exit potential (acquisition targets, IPO potential, strategic buyers)
- **Investment Timeline**: Recommended timeline (short-term 1-2 years, medium 3-5 years, long-term 5+ years)

### 12. **Metadata**
- **Confidence Score**: 0-1 confidence in report accuracy
- **Data Quality Notes**: What's based on evidence vs. inference, what's missing

## Investment Decision Support

This report provides everything an investor needs to make an informed decision:

✅ **Quick Understanding**: Executive summary for busy investors  
✅ **Investment Case**: Clear investment thesis  
✅ **Risk Assessment**: Comprehensive risk analysis with mitigation strategies  
✅ **Financial Analysis**: Revenue, profitability, and scale indicators  
✅ **Market Intelligence**: Competitive landscape and market position  
✅ **Growth Potential**: Expansion opportunities and market trends  
✅ **Valuation Inputs**: Factors for valuation modeling  
✅ **Exit Strategy**: Potential exit paths and timeline  

## Quality Standards

- **Evidence-Based**: All conclusions backed by website evidence
- **Honest Assessment**: Clear about what's known vs. inferred
- **Investment-Focused**: Written for investors, not general audiences
- **Comprehensive**: Covers all critical investment dimensions
- **Actionable**: Provides specific insights for decision-making
