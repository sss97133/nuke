┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                      MULTI-VEHICLE AUCTION LOTS                             │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

    LOT TYPES
    ─────────
    
    1. SINGLE VEHICLE (Traditional)
       • One vehicle per auction
       • Standard flow
       • Any tier
    
    2. MULTI-VEHICLE SEQUENTIAL
       • Multiple vehicles, one after another
       • Each has its own auction window
       • Example: 5 cars, 10 minutes each = 50 minutes total
    
    3. SIMULTANEOUS
       • Multiple vehicles, all end at same time
       • Buyers can bid on multiple
       • Example: 10 cars, all end at 2:00 PM
    
    4. RAPID-FIRE (SSS Tier)
       • Ultra-fast, one after another
       • 3 minutes per vehicle
       • No sniping protection
       • No reserves
       • Example: 14 cars = 42 minutes total
       • Example: 140 cars = 7 hours total
       • Example: 1400 cars = 70 hours total (3 days)
