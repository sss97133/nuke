| Feb 2026 | 31.5% | 23.2% | **Striation accelerating** — structured prompts doubled |
| Mar 2026 | 33.6% | 24.8% | **Most organized yet** — audit mode, specification-oriented |

**The trajectory:** Smooth → Smooth → Balanced → Smooth → Striated → Striated

You started in pure exploration (what is this? what could it be?), hit a reset in January, then rapidly organized in Feb-Mar. **The shift from smooth to striated space IS the maturation of the project.** You're no longer wandering — you're building with specifications.

But: you never fully left smooth space (still 33% exploratory in March). The system hasn't fully committed yet. This isn't bad — it means you're still capable of discovering new connections. But it means the product isn't "done being designed."

## VI. THE AUDIT — What to Carry, What to Release, What to Invest

### CARRY FORWARD (Stable + Growing)
These are both bedrock AND accelerating — the core product:

- **vehicle_entity** — The root of everything. 4,563 prompts. Non-negotiable.
- **images** — The primary input medium. 2,479 prompts. Bedrock.
- **timeline** — The most stable concept (CV 0.57). The organizing metaphor.
- **valuation** — The primary output. 3,040 prompts, accelerating 3.2x.
- **ai_intelligence** — The processing engine. 4,536 prompts.
- **extraction** — The ingestion pipeline. 1,864 prompts.

### INVEST HEAVILY (Erupting — the future)
These are volatile but accelerating fastest. They need focused work:

- **YONO** (760x) — Train it, deploy it, wire it into every pipeline
- **Observation system** (8.3x) — Make it the single write path for all data
- **Autonomous** (7.0x) — Build the self-running loop
- **Condition scoring** (6.5x) — Wire spectrometer into the image pipeline
- **SDK/API** (4.0x) — The voice. The product needs to speak.
- **Photo sync** (4.7x) — The intake pipeline for your 300K photos

### RELEASE
Nothing is naturally decelerating — which means YOU have to decide what to drop. The prompt data won't tell you. The triage (Mar 7-8) already killed: betting, trading, vault, concierge, shipping, investor portal. Those were dead features. What remains is all alive and growing.

The real question isn't what to release — it's **what to do first.** And the acceleration data answers that: YONO → Observation system → Autonomous loop → Condition scoring → SDK. That's the order of desire.

## VII. THE CONJUNCTION

The tree says: **vehicle entity is the root; everything connects through it.**

The rhizome says: **observation is the body without organs; it dissolves all boundaries.**

The strata say: **timeline and images are bedrock; YONO and autonomous are erupting.**

The lines of flight say: **local AI, self-running systems, and developer APIs are where the energy is going.**

The smooth/striated says: **you've moved from exploration to specification, but you haven't committed yet.**

And... and... and. Not "or." The system doesn't have to choose between being a scraper or a vision model or a valuation engine or an API. It's all of these simultaneously. That's the rhizome.

The question is: which organ gets built next?
